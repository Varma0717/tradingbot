Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TradeMantra

How do I get started with TradeMantra?

Simply sign up for a free account to start with paper trading. You can upgrade to our Pro plan anytime to access live trading and advanced features. Our onboarding process guides you through connecting your broker account and setting up your first trading strategy.

Which brokers do you support?

We support major Indian brokers including Zerodha, Angel Broking, ICICI Direct, HDFC Securities, and many others. Our platform integrates seamlessly with their APIs for real-time trading execution.

Is my trading data secure?

Yes, absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption and security measures to protect your data. We never store your broker passwords, and all API communications are encrypted. Your trading strategies and performance data are completely private.

Can I customize trading strategies?

Our Pro plan includes access to strategy parameters that you can adjust. For Enterprise customers, we offer completely custom strategy development based on your specific requirements and trading style.

What support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive support including email support for all users, priority support for Pro subscribers, and dedicated account management for Enterprise customers. We also provide extensive documentation, video tutorials, and regular webinars.
